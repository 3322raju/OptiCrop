🌱 About OptiCrop

OptiCrop is an Artificial Intelligence based Smart Agricultural Production Optimization Engine.

The system predicts the most suitable crop using:

  • 🌾 Nitrogen (N)
  • 🌿 Phosphorus (P)
  • 🍃 Potassium (K)
  • 🌡 Temperature
  • 💧 Humidity
  • ⚗ Soil pH
  • 🌧 Rainfall

Our objective is to help farmers increase productivity, reduce crop failure, and support sustainable agriculture using Machine Learning.
